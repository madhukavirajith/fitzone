Inspiration: How Dinuka Lost 20kg & Found True Confidence

Published on June 4, 2026 | By Trainer Sarath Perera

Here at FitZone Fitness Center in Kurunegala, we see transformations every day. But some journeys are so inspiring that they deserve to be shared. Today, we're highlighting **Dinuka Lakmal**, a local member who made the decision to change his health, lost 20 kilograms in 6 months, and completely turned his life around.

The Challenge

Six months ago, Dinuka was struggling with low energy, poor stamina, and weight-related joint pain. Working a desk job, he had fallen into a sedentary lifestyle. "I felt tired all the time," Dinuka says. "Climbing a flight of stairs left me out of breath, and I knew I had to make a change before my health deteriorated further."

The FitZone Strategy

Dinuka signed up for the FitZone **Premium Plan**, which gave him access to our cardio classes and 1-on-1 personal training. Here is how we structured his transformation:

  • Step 1: Functional Cardio. Trainer Sarath set up a progressive cardio routine, starting with 20 minutes of moderate treadmill walks, slowly building up to high-intensity interval training (HIIT) on the spin bikes.
  • Step 2: Core & Strength Training. To protect Dinuka's joints and increase his metabolic rate, we added 3 days of strength coaching, focusing on core stability, deadlifts, and overhead presses.
  • Step 3: Nutritional Adjustments. We worked together to swap deep-fried foods and excess refined sugars for lean proteins, fresh gotukola, brown rice, and healthy snacks like almonds.

The Results

With consistency, discipline, and the support of the FitZone community, Dinuka achieved what seemed impossible:

  • Weight Lost: 20 kg (from 98kg down to 78kg).
  • Stamina: Can now run 5km easily without joint pain.
  • Confidence: "I look better, feel stronger, and my confidence has skyrocketed. Joining FitZone was the best decision I ever made."
"The trainers didn't just give me a workout plan. They taught me how to move correctly, how to eat, and supported me whenever I felt like giving up. The community is like family."

Dinuka's Advice to You

"Don't wait for next week or next month. The first step is the hardest, but once you walk through those doors and start moving, your body and mind will thank you. If I can do it, anyone can!"
