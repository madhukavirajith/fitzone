Fueling Success: Healthy Recipes & Complete Meal Plans

Published on June 4, 2026 | By Nutritionist Harini Bandara

Training at the gym is only half the battle. If your nutrition isn't aligned with your goals, you're missing out on the majority of your potential progress. Whether you want to lose body fat or build lean muscle, your food is the fuel. Here is a breakdown of simple recipes and a complete daily meal plan structure to get you started.

Top 3 Quick & Healthy Recipes

1. High-Protein Sri Lankan Chicken Curry (Fat Loss/Muscle Gain)

A classic, modified to reduce excess fats while keeping the aromatic spices and high protein profile.

  • Ingredients: 500g skinless chicken breast (cubed), 1 tbsp coconut oil, 1 sliced onion, curry leaves, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, unroasted curry powder, 1/2 cup light coconut milk.
  • Instructions: Heat oil in a pan, sauté onions, ginger-garlic paste, and curry leaves. Add spiced chicken and cook until seared. Stir in light coconut milk, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes. Serve with brown rice or red rice.

2. Post-Workout Energy Smoothie

Perfect for recovering after a tough cardio or strength training class at FitZone.

  • Ingredients: 1 scoop whey protein (vanilla or chocolate), 1 ripe banana, 1 tbsp peanut butter, 1 cup unsweetened almond or soy milk, a pinch of cinnamon.
  • Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Drink within 45 minutes of completing your workout.

3. FitZone Egg & Vegetable Scramble

A low-calorie, nutrient-dense breakfast to start your day strong.

  • Ingredients: 2 whole eggs, 2 egg whites, spinach, diced bell peppers, tomatoes, salt, black pepper.
  • Instructions: Whisk the eggs and whites together. Sauté vegetables in a non-stick pan with a tiny spray of olive oil. Pour in the eggs, scramble until cooked, and season with fresh black pepper.
